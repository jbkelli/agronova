AgriNova Bio

Innovate. Grow. Sustain.

Welcome to AgriNova Solutions Group, an association based in Kanyekini Ward, Kirinyaga Central Subcounty, dedicated to transforming African agriculture.

Our Purpose

We were created to empower agripreneurs and to cultivate a future where African agriculture is synonymous with innovation, sustainability, and prosperity for all.

What We Do

  • Enhancing agricultural productivity.
  • Promoting innovative and sustainable value addition and agro-processing.
  • Strengthening market access for agricultural produce.
  • Fostering agripreneurship and creating employment.
  • Championing gender inclusivity and empowering youth in agripreneurship.

The Benefit (Our Goal)

Our strategic goal is to become the indispensable partner for 1 million African agripreneurs by 2030.
